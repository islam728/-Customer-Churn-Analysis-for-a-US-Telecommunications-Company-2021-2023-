## Executive Summary
This project presents a customer churn analysis for a US telecommunications company using data from 2021 to 2023. The primary goal is to understand the key factors driving customer attrition and support data-driven strategies to improve customer retention.

This is a beginner-level Power BI project completed as part of a learning course and approved for public sharing.

---

## Business Objectives
- Analyze customer churn trends across multiple years  
- Identify key drivers of customer attrition  
- Assess the impact of phone bundles and service usage on churn  
- Provide actionable insights to support retention strategies  

---

## Tools & Skills
- Power BI  
- Data Visualization & KPI Analysis  
- Basic Data Modeling  
- Business-Oriented Data Analysis  

---

## Key Insights (Examples)
- Certain customer segments consistently show higher churn rates  
- Specific phone bundles and service combinations are associated with increased churn  
- Churn trends vary year-over-year, reflecting changes in customer behavior  
- Customers with bundled services tend to have higher retention rates  

---

## Recommended Business Actions
1. **Target High-Risk Segments**  
   Focus retention campaigns on customer groups with the highest likelihood of churn.

2. **Optimize Phone Bundles**  
   Redesign underperforming bundles based on customer usage patterns and preferences.

3. **Proactive Retention Strategy**  
   Use early churn indicators such as reduced usage to intervene before customers leave.

4. **Customer Loyalty Programs**  
   Offer incentives and personalized promotions to long-term customers.

5. **Ongoing Performance Monitoring**  
   Track churn KPIs regularly to evaluate the effectiveness of retention initiatives.
